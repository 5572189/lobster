Jonathan于1996年由英国移居香港，至今已于亚洲扎根约22年。初来港时，曾任酒店管理课程讲师的他，现在偶尔亦会重拾教鞭当客席讲师。 身为资深餐饮业专才及企业家，先後于英国及亚洲开设不少餐厅及成立零售及市场营销机构。 The Butchers Club最初在2013年5月开设于香港仔工业区。约二千呎的空间变为风平房，其后更于厨房中央加设一张主厨桌，以满足肉食迷。尽管地理位置不算便利但承蒙客人爱戴，等候订座也需轮候六个月。 Jonathan亦为自学切肉师及鱼商，更是The Butchers Club背后的灵魂人物。懷抱雄心壮志的他，熱切期望未来可以带领The Butchers Club冲出国际及登陆中国市场

Jonathan, originally from the UK moved to Hong Kong in 1996 and has now been in Asia for almost 22 years. He originally arrived in the territory as a lecturer in Hotel Management and still does the occasional guest lecture today. He is seasoned F&B professional and serial entrepreneur having founded and built numerous restaurant, retail and marketing organisations in UK and Asia. The original Butchers Club opened in May 2013 in an Industrial area in Aberdeen, Hong Kong. After transforming the 2,000-square-foot space into a dry-aging facility. He also added a chef’s table in the middle of the kitchen catering especially to meat-loving customers. The location wasn’t ideal, but there was soon a six-month waiting list. Jonathan is a self-taught butcher and fish monger and was the main inspiration behind The Butchers Club brand. He has huge aspirations and plans to take The Butchers Club global in the near future and the expansion into China excites him greatly.
